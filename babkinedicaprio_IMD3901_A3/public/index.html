What I did

For this assignment, I created a virtual blackjack game! Pick your role, dealer or player. Instructions on screen will guide you through the game!

What was challenging

Understanding how to relay information between the server and the client was quite tricky at first. Furthermore, designing networking architectures requires quite a lot of planning, if I had to do it again, I would sit down with pen and paper and define my functions and database structure before coding.

What went well

Once I understood how socket.io works, it was fun and predictable to develop a project using it.
